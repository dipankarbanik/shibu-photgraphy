Introduction

Most things in life are moments which are a lifetime of remembrance. There will be a time in life when you want to revive your past celebrations and live again all good times you spend with your loved ones. The best thing about a picture is that it never changes, even when the people in it do. A photo for me is not just an image but it’s a feeling, a story and a moment which is captured for a lifetime. I am Shibankar and I love to create images for my clients that retain their strength and impact over the years, regardless of the number of times they are viewed..
